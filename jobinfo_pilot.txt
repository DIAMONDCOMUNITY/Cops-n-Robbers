
  JOB IDEA: Pilot
  
    - Overview
      Players can go on duty as either an airline pilot, or helicopter pilot.
    
    - How to Obtain
      Players can go on duty at any airport or public helicopter pad.
    
    - How to Cancel/End Duty
      Leave the aircraft behind or return to any helipad/airport
    
    - Missions
      Players in airlines will receive an itinerary of when to pick up
      at which airports and what schedules. Making your next destination
      on time will result in being paid. Being late only gives 10% payout.
      
      Players in Helicopters will receive random calls to pick up NPCs.
      The NPCs will then ask to be taken to another location.
      
    - Special Notes
      Players can also request a pilot to pick them up.

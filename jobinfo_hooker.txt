
  JOB IDEA: Escort Service
  
    - Overview
      Players can offer sex to each other for cash.
    
    - How to Obtain
      Start prostitute duty at any gentlemen's club.
    
    - How to Cancel/End Duty
      To end duty, return to any gentlemen's club.
    
    - Missions
      While this job allows people to offer sex to players for money,
      there are chances for missions.
      
      Players will get requests by high profile individuals.
      Players respond to these high profile people, and perform the tasks.
      The high profile individuals may be police officers undercover.
      These tasks will be high paying.
      
    - Special Notes
      Players can contract diseases from NPCs and infected players.
      Two uninfected players won't be able to infect each other.
      STDs cannot be cured but their damage can be mitigated.
      
      Players can pick up NPC hookers at any time when they're working
      As long as no other peds see them, they won't become wanted

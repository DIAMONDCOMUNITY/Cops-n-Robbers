What is Cops and Robbers ('CNR')?

CNR is a casual game where you, the player, can either commit crimes and become wanted, or join the police force and go after wanted players! Alternatively, if you're just a boring no-lifer like the kind of guy who sits at home for several hours a day writing game scripts for free (send help please), you can just stay innocent and work legal jobs to earn cash for cars, houses, etc.

Our goal is to give you the opportunity to play our take on an online GTA game, while letting players be the cops. As you gain rank in being a civilian, you'll earn more money for doing actions in game. As you gain rank as a cop, you'll gain access to better duty weapons, police vehicles, and even be able to go on duty as SWAT.

Rob gas stations, do a bank heist, sleep with a hooker, sell drugs, do drugs, drive drunk, buy a bar, become a home owner, chase bad guys, kill cops, fly a plane, skydive, drive your partner off the cliff at Mount Chiliad... Your only limit is your imagination - and the time I have to script things.

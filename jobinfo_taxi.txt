
  JOB IDEA: Taxi Driver
  
    - Overview
      Players can go on duty as a taxi driver.
      Taxi drivers transport people around the map.
    
    - How to Obtain
      Go on duty at any of the axi locations
    
    - How to Cancel/End Duty
      Leave the taxi behind or return it to one of the taxi depots
    
    - Missions
      Players will receive calls to go pick up NPCs for cash. Paid on dropoff.
      Players will also get flagdowns. Paid on dropoff.
      
    - Special Notes
